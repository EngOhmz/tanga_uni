What We Do Together

Tanga University Foundation brings together university students and graduates to unite their efforts in charity, volunteering, and youth-driven initiatives. We work together for community empowerment, social change, and sustainable development across Tanga and beyond.

Unity & Collaboration

We bring together students and graduates from various universities to collaborate on community-driven projects, empowering youth to lead and make a lasting impact.

Charity & Volunteering

Our foundation organizes charity events and volunteering opportunities for students to give back to their communities and make a positive difference in the lives of others.

Youth Empowerment

We provide platforms for youth to engage in activities that address local issues and contribute to community development, focusing on initiatives that require the energy and creativity of the younger generation.

Regional Development

Through sustainable projects, we engage students and graduates to contribute towards regional development in Tanga, promoting educational growth, environmental protection, and economic progress.

Humanitarian Support

We organize and participate in humanitarian projects that provide support to vulnerable communities, ensuring no one is left behind in the quest for a better, more equitable society.

Educational Support

We provide academics support, learning materials, and mentoring to ensure that students from all backgrounds, especially in Tanga, can access quality education and achieve their full potential.

Our Events & Activities

We actively engage in events that empower students and uplift communities across Tanga.

Environmental Cleanups

Team participate in cleaning campaigns to promote a cleaner, greener Tanga region.

Knowledge Seminars

We organize seminars on leadership, innovation, and career development for youth empowerment.

Community Outreach

We extend support to rural schools and families, delivering essentials and mentorship programs.
