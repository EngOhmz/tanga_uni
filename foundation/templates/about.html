About Tanga Uni Foundation

Empowering Communities Through Education, Unity, and Leadership

Who We Are

Tanga Uni Foundation is a youth-led non-profit organization established to foster educational support, community service, leadership development, and unity among university students and the Tanga region at large.

  • Founded by students and alumni from various universities in Tanzania.
  • Operates with a vision to uplift society through knowledge sharing and active participation.
  • Encourages responsible leadership, mentorship, and social accountability.

We believe in the power of unity and knowledge to transform lives. By connecting students, professionals, and community members, we aim to build a strong network of changemakers who will shape the future of Tanga and Tanzania.

Our Mission

To unite university students and alumni for the development of Tanga through impactful projects, leadership initiatives, and academic support.

Our Vision

To be a leading platform for academic excellence, leadership growth, and community empowerment within and beyond the Tanga region.

Core Goals

  • ✅ Promote education and mentorship.
  • ✅ Engage in community outreach programs.
  • ✅ Foster leadership, creativity, and innovation.
  • ✅ Create platforms for networking and skills sharing.
